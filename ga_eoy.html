GENERAL ASSEMBLY | END OF YEAR CAMPAIGN

Advertising | Campaign Design

End of Year marketing campaign for General Assembly to drive demand for the education company's part-time courses and Q4 revenue. The messaging of the campaign encourages prospective students to take advantage of holiday down time and use the break to enroll in one of GA's 1-week Remote courses.

The campaign features an overhead shot of someone taking a Remote course while the background visuals cycle through holiday imagery, day-to-day busy life, and career illustrations. The core message "Take {Remote} Control of Your Time/Holiday/Career" empowers consumers to give themselves the gift of learning during the hectic holiday season.
