GOWANUS

Branding | Logo design | Editorial design

Project for the Brand Identity Design course at School of Visual Arts. Assignment was to create an identity for a New York City neighborhood. Gowanus is a Brooklyn neighborhood located along the Gowanus Canal. The logo serves as a call-to-action, made up of lines that represent the canal.

The "O" of the logo is used as a graphic element, representing an open portal to the neighborhood.
