Yieldstreet for Basquiat: King Pleasure

Branding | Partnership Event Design

Branded sponsorship and event design for the Basquiat: King Pleasure exhibition, featuring 200 never before seen works. Yieldstreet, a fintech alternative investment platform, co-sponsored the exhibit in order to target to high net worth individuals interested in art. The startup allows users to diversify their portfolios by investing in asset classes previously only available to the uber-wealthy, including art.

I led the art direction and branding of the event from digital signage to a giveaway swag item used to capture emails. The line "Appreciate art that appreciates" plays to the art audience while nodding to the key benefit of investing in assets that will grow your portfolio over time. I also concepted three different givewaway items including enamel pins, posters, and a puzzle all based around Jean-Michel Basquiat's art.
