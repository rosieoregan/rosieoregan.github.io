GENERAL ASSEMBLY 10 YEAR ANNIVERSARY

Marketing Campaign

Marketing campaign celebrating 10 years since General Assembly was founded in 2011. The creative highlights how in the past decade, GA has grown from a co-working space in New York City to a global leader in education. The design direction is heavily influenced by "Old School GA", leaning on the brand's primary color palette of red, black, and white. The mosaic colorblocking illustrates how GA has built a community putting people first.

The lander and e-book are additional collateral pieces created to drive acquisition for the campaign.
