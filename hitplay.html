GENERAL ASSEMBLY HIT PLAY MARKETING CAMPAIGN

Campaign concept & execution

Quarterly marketing campaign for General Assembly. We kicked off 2021 by producing themed conferences to build new audiences and reach our existing user. "Hit Play" speaks to how easy GA makes it to get started on a new career, to pursue a new interest, or to engage with thought leaders virtually.

The colorful gradients contrast with the grid background and the sticker elements were used to cue the different conference names.
