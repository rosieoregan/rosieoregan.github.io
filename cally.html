Branding | Logo design

Nimble brand identity project for Cally, a startup that leverages team science and data science to help people collaborate better. After landing on a new strategy that led to a new brand name, Cally was in need of a reimagined logo and brandmark.

The brandmark and graphic element is an elephant, to represent teamwork and collaboration. Elephants are unique in that they have symbiotic relationships with different species of animals, including egrets and baboons.
