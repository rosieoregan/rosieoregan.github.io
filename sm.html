STRONGMINDS

Brand & logo refinement | Collateral design | Social design

Brand identity and collateral design for StrongMinds, a nonprofit that provides group talk therapy to women in Africa so they can alleviate depression and lift themselves out of poverty. StrongMinds' model is designed to treat depression rapidly, cost-effectively, and at scale. The organization needed a brand refresh that was authentic in order to speak to their female patients, as well as professional to attract the investor community.

Drawing from the brush stroke line and letter "i" of the original logo, the graphic elements are reminiscent of a community coming together, just as StrongMinds' talk therapy helps women heal through their collective strength. The custom icons of African women's profiles are used to help the brand communicate a more human, authentic tone.
